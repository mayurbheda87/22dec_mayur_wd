(4) What are HTML Entities?

Ans:Some characters are reserved in HTML and they have
special meaning when used in HTML document.For example,
you cannot use the greater than and less than signs or angle brackets
within your HTML text because the browser will treat them differently and
will try to draw a meaning related to HTML tag.

Example of HTML Entities:

and-sign nbsp; - non-breaking space

and-sign lt; - less than sign

and-sign gt; - greater than sign
