(5) What are different types of lists in HTML?

Ans:There are three types of lists in HTML:

Unordered list or Bulleted list (ul).
Ordered list or Numbered list (ol).
Description list or Definition list (dl).

Ex. of ul tag:

<ul>
<li>Apple</li>
<li>Mango</li>
<li>Banana</li>
<li>Grapes</li>
<li>Orange</li>
</ul>

Ex. of ol tag:

<ol>
<li>Apple</li>
<li>Mango</li>
<li>Banana</li>
<li>Grapes</li>
<li>Orange</li>
</ol>

Ex. of dl tag:

<dl>
<dt>Apple</dt>
<dd>Mango</dd>
<dt>Banana</dt>
<dd>Grapes</dd>
<dt>Orange</dt>
</dl>
